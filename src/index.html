About me

Writing clean code and a love for component driven HTML5 and CSS3 gets me going. I graduated as a visual designer, but I decided that pushing pixels by code is way more exciting than Photoshop. With my background in design, I navigate perfectly between the developers and the designers.

  • Sass, Less
  • BEM
  • Bootstrap
  • Umbraco CMS
  • Photoshop, Illustrator, Sketch, InVision

I spend my spare time hiking in the Dutch woods, doing yoga and reading. Not just non-fiction, I love books on spirituality, (neuro) psychology and personal development. I am a certified Neuro Linguistic Programmer. My favourite music is Britpop (think Oasis, The Verve and Kasabian) and ‘60’s/’70’s.
